Describing the Problem

Understanding the Challenges:

One of the biggest challenges for job seekers, especially students and recent graduates, is the lack of tailored feedback when crafting their resumes. Traditional resume-building platforms offer generic templates, but they often fail to provide industry-specific insights, leaving applicants unsure about how to optimize their resumes for tech roles. Additionally, many students from underrepresented backgrounds may not have access to professional career guidance, making it even more difficult to create compelling resumes that stand out.

Gaps in Existing Solutions:

While numerous online platforms exist to help individuals generate resumes, few focus on optimizing them based on real job descriptions and industry expectations. Most resume-building tools provide static templates without dynamic, AI-driven recommendations. This lack of personalization results in generic resumes that may not align with what hiring managers in tech companies are looking for. Additionally, there is a scarcity of tools that can provide real-time feedback, making it difficult for users to iteratively improve their resumes.

Attempts to Solve the Problem:

To accommodate for the massive demand to create resumes online, countless web pages have been made, providing the tools and resources for people to create their own resume. But there are little to no sources or apps that provide an engine to help people build resumes in real-time. However, there have been attempts made to fill that demand. A web page like Enchanv has been created, providing users an AI engine to utilize making formidable, credible, and impactful resumes.

Describing our App

Who are the users of the application?

The primary users of the Tech Industry Resume Optimization Assistant will be college students seeking to break into the tech industry, particularly those aiming for internships or entry-level roles. These users will have varying levels of experience with resume building and would include students from diverse academic, socioeconomic, and cultural backgrounds.

This app could have a significant positive impact on minority groups by providing guidance tailored to highlight relevant skills and experiences, even for students without access to professional career services or mentorship. Our app would empower underrepresented groups with tools to compete for positions where they might otherwise face systemic barriers, such as lack of professional networks.

What will the users do with the web app?

Users will interact with the Tech Industry Resume Optimization Assistant to enhance their resumes specifically for tech industry roles. They will engage with features designed to analyze, critique, and provide recommendations for improving their resumes based on the demands of tech recruiters.

Our web app will include the following features:

  1. Resume Analysis Tool: Users will upload their resumes, and the app will analyze the content using AI-powered algorithms. It will identify strengths and weaknesses, such as missing technical skills, unclear role descriptions, or formatting inconsistencies.
  2. Job-Specific Optimization: Users can input a job description or keywords for a specific role. The app will compare their resume against these requirements and recommend targeted changes to better align with the desired position.
  3. Resume Template and Content Builder: For students starting from scratch or looking to overhaul their resume, the app will offer customizable templates and guided content generation. Prompts will help users structure sections like "Skills," "Experience," and "Projects."

What kind of information will the users be viewing?

The information the users will receive will involve any actions, classes, or skills they should build to improve their resumes. Class information will be pulled from the CSE courses and the Informatics courses information websites. The users will also be viewing data from Gemini's AI API (which we have tested in the provided CORS test), which will be utilized to give more personalized responses to users looking to optimize their resumes. Additionally, a copy of the Foster Resume Template will be provided.
